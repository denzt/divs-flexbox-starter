Hi. I'm Teresa.

I am a Responsive Designer and Developer who thrives on visual storytelling. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in the rabbit hole of blogs and books, I can be found traveling, hiking and searching for new stories.

I was born in Owensboro, Kentucky and raised in Greenwood, Indiana. I love to travel and have moved around from upstate New York back to the midwest in the Windy City of Chicago. My family is spread out all over America so I suppose that is where I owe some of my love for traveling.
My passion: I LOVE fine arts, especially painting in acrylic and oils. I enjoy making larger than life paintings and hope to show the world one day. I find myself immersed in visual, audio, and performing arts. These are all excellent and therapeutic ways to handle life.
A Fun Fact: I am obsessed with animals particularly cats! They have been a part of my life since I can remember. I currently have 2 cats living with me. However, I'm not a crazy cat lady... yet.
